Welcome to your guide to Amsterdam’s vintage fashion scene.

From hidden boutiques to lively flea markets, this website helps you explore the city’s unique retro culture. Whether you’re a local or a visitor, you’ll find inspiration, practical tips, and must-visit shops.

about this

website

This site is designed to introduce you to the world of vintage fashion in Amsterdam. It offers curated guides, stories, and tips so you can easily navigate the city’s best second-hand treasures.

why vintage in

amsterdam

Amsterdam is known for its creative neighborhoods and diverse style. Vintage fashion here is more than clothing, it’s part of the city’s culture, blending sustainability, individuality, and history.

how to

explore

Each page of this website points you to a new part of the vintage experience. Dive into the Shops page to see the best vintage shops, explore Markets page, or learn more in the About section.
